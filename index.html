Short Biography

I am currently a third-year Ph.D. student at Rutgers, The State University of New Jersey, New Brunswick, advised by Prof. Yingying Chen. I obtained my Master's degree of Computer Science at Rutgers, the State University of New Jersey, New Brunswick and Bachelor's degree of Information Security from Huazhong University of Science and Technology, Wuhan. My research interests include mobile computing, mobile and wireless sensing, deep learning security and smart healthcare.
